Contacting Support
  • Django WebRemote Website

Changing Your Current Password
1

Open the app and log in.

2

Go to "Settings" then "Account Settings".

3

Click on "Change Password".

4

Enter your current password and the new password.

5

Click "Save Changes" to apply the new password.

Updating Your Profile
1

Open the app and log in to your account

2

Go to "Settings" then "Account Settings"

3

Click on "Update Profile"

4

Enter the new information you want to update

5

Click "Save Changes" to apply the updates
