About Django WebRemote

Django WebRemote was built with accessibility in mind — a simple, web-based remote designed for people who struggle with traditional remotes.

Why Django WebRemote?
Django WebRemote is a web-based remote control for your smart TV, designed to be simple and easy to use. Django WebRemote was created to help people who struggle with traditional remotes, especially older adults or those with limited mobility. It offers a simple, customizable interface to control your smart TV using just a few large, clear buttons.

Why We Started

Django WebRemote started as a way to help my grandfather, who had a stroke and now has difficulty using voice commands or small remotes. The idea was to give him an easier way to control the TV — just by tapping a few large buttons on a tablet or browser.

Who This is For

It quickly became clear that Django WebRemote could help more people: seniors, those with limited mobility, or anyone who just wants a more intuitive way to navigate their smart TV.

Where It's Going

The remote is customizable, easy to use, and works on any device with a browser. It's still evolving — but the heart of the project remains the same: build something simple, helpful, and accessible.

Help Support Us

Django WebRemote is a passion project built to make technology more accessible. If you'd like to support the mission — whether through a small donation, sharing feedback, or contributing code — your help truly makes a difference. Every bit of support helps us improve and reach more people who need it.
